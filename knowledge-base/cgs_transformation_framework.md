# CGS Transformation Framework

## Overview
The CGS Transformation Framework operates across three levels:
- UNDERSTAND (Industry Level): Recognising technological discontinuity, 
  new business models, shifts in competition
- TRANSFORM (Firm Level): Formulate strategy, assess capabilities, 
  launch or adjust products and services  
- LEAD (Executive Level): Envision disruptive potential, assess 
  capability gaps, invest to implement the response

## The Three Cs
Every client engagement addresses gaps in:
- CLARITY: Does the leadership team have a shared, clear view of 
  where the industry is going?
- CAPABILITY: Does the organisation have the skills, processes and 
  technology to execute the strategy?
- CONVICTION: Does leadership have the courage and alignment to 
  act decisively and overcome inertia?

## Inertia Types
- Dominant Logic Inertia (Executive Level): Leaders cannot see 
  past existing mental models
- Structural Inertia (Firm Level): Processes, incentives and 
  org design resist change
